The categories of Cyberfare:  5. Local Attractions

 

Description of our local community:

     Kaohsiung is located at the southernmost tip of Taiwan. In 1979, it included 11 districts including Yancheng, Gushan, Zuoying, Nanzi, Sanmin, Xinxing, Qianjin, Qianzhen, Qijin, and Xiaogang. These areas total 35 districts, including 27 townships and cities, including former Kaohsiung County, Fengshan, Okayama, Qishan, Mino and Binlin. Kaohsiung has bright sunshine all year round and a pleasant climate. The people are naive and enthusiastic. This city with mountains, rivers, oceans, ports and airports has successfully used its rich historical humanities, mountains and marine resources to develop into a potential tourist city, many popular attractions and historical heritage, worth exploring and leaving behind beautiful Memories.

 

Summary of the case study plan:

     Kaohsiung's historical monuments are historical places of historical development in Kaohsiung. They have rich cultural heritage, and there are many historical monuments, temples or historic sites left over. During the holidays, tourists enjoy visiting Kaohsiung, exploring its culture and history, and tasting delicious food. All this enriches our cultural content in Kaohsiung. Get opinions and suggestions about Kaohsiung by interviewing members of parliament, residents, etc. Through our website, we hope readers can learn more about the history of Kaohsiung and learn to cherish and protect it.
