The PIER-2 ART CENTER

 

The arts center was originally an abandoned warehouse site due to Kaohsiung's shift from industrial city to service city. The warehouses were built in 1973. Due to the persistence of local artists, the area was finally released and remade to be an arts center. In 2006, the Bureau of Cultural Affairs of Kaohsiung City Government and under management of the Kaohsiung Pier-2 Art Development Association and Shu-Te University's art development workshop took over the center, starting a series of exhibitions.

 

The Warehouse Cluster Art Zone is located next to the pier. In addition to vehicles and ships, the people coming in and out are not only the crowds but also the sea breeze. The open view of the sea and sky is like the vastness of thoughts. In the conflict at the junction of sea and land, what is broad is the dazzling future.

 

 

Liouhe Night Market

 

The Liouhe Night Market is a tourist night market in Sinsing District, Kaohsiung, Taiwan. It is one of the most popular markets in Taiwan where seafood, handicrafts, clothing, knives, cameras and live animals are sold.

 

In the 1950s, there had been many food stalls stationed in Dagangpu area of Sinsing District collectively known as Dagangpu Night Market . Since then, the night market has been developed into a large-scale market known as Liouhe Night Market.

The night market is accessible by walking distance west from exits 1, 9, and 11 of the Formosa Boulevard Station of the Kaohsiung MRT.

Wannian Season

 

The most important blessing activity in the event is the "Fire Lion" event. The fire lion was formerly known as the "Black Tiger General", the mount of Emperor Baosheng. It is a religious ceremony to celebrate the birthday of Emperor Baosheng. During the event, the participants hung the blessing cards on the base of the fire lion. During the fire lion retreat ceremony on the last day of the fire lion, they put the  blessing cards in the fire lion golden furnace specially made this year to burn the blessing cards and put the wishes of the people.

 

Through the burning process of the blessing card, it will reach heaven and listen, and bring the wishes of all living beings to heaven. Stepping on the street through a parade, full of prayers along the area, turning into lights of hope and lighting up the night sky, can be said to be the most important climax of the event, and it is also the most anticipated moment in the Wannian Season.
