Participation motivation

 

We participate in clubs, and teachers encourage everyone to participate in the 2025   Cyberfair.. This year's theme is to show Taiwan's life and vitality. We are in the first grade. Everyone is participating in an online blog competition for the first time. Everything is rusty. After waiting for the task to be completed, we find that we have learned a lot and broadened our horizons. Our school is located near the Zuoying District of Kaohsiung, so after school, we occasionally meet some foreign and foreign tourists. This sparked our interest in doing something for our hometown. Why don't we set up a website to introduce this historic place and let more people come here? Classmates work together, work together, and are determined to do it well.

 

During the winter vacation, there was a Chiikawa exhibition in Kaohsiung Port, a market in Pier-2 Art, and a temple fair in Zuoying, so we divided into groups and divided the work. Each group had a responsible student to take pictures and remind students to record videos because they had to make videos. At the same time, the web design students also started to make web pages. The instructor discussed the progress of web page creation with students through the LINE group on their mobile phones. Understand the progress of students in completing the task of taking photos at various tourist attractions.
