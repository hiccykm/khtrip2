Chinese chess cake

Fire lion cake

 

The Fire Lion Cake came from the blessing ceremony of the Wanying Season (Welcoming Fire Lion) since 2001. According to legend, the Fire Lion Cake can fulfill the wishes, bringing luck and the Fire Lion Cake uses the lotus seeds in the lotus pond as the ingredients. Fire Lion welcomes happiness, Naxi and other meanings. In addition to enjoying the unique taste and flavor of fire lion cake, customers can also pray for luck and peace.
